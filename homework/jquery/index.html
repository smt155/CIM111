Miami's Paradise: Golden Beach

By: Sheila Taratuta

A private town within a town. Golden Beach is home to some of the most luxurious homes in South Florida. During the 1920's two brothers bought the property now known as Golden Beach and found investors. On May 23rd, 1929, Golden Beach was established as a Town. The town was created with a vision to create a tight knit community along the beautiful ocean and intercoastal of Miami. As the years went by, and different architectrual styles arouse, the community began to expand and build beautiful luxurious homes.
